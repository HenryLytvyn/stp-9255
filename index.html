About the Game

Fairy Legends: Tail Quest

Enter an era of war, betrayal, and resilience in Dynasty: Fallen & Rise, a tactical strategy RPG where your choices shape the fate of an empire.

Play as an exiled heir fighting to reclaim the throne of your fallen kingdom. Build alliances, master battlefield tactics, and command legendary heroes in your quest to unite the land once more.

Craft your legacy in a world where every sword stroke echoes through history.

  • Rebuild your dynasty from ruin.

  • Command armies in real-time battles.

  • Outsmart rivals in political intrigue and strategy.
